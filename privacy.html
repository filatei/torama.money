1. Introduction

This Policy explains how Torama Global Services Ltd ("Torama", "we") collects and uses personal data across our products — Torama Vote, Torama eTax, Phidex, Otuburu, BibiTrust and the Situation Room. We process data in line with the Nigeria Data Protection Act and applicable financial-services rules.

2. Data we collect

  • Account data — name, email, phone number, password and authentication credentials.
  • Identity data — government ID, date of birth, BVN and bank-account details, where required for verification.
  • Transaction data — wallet balances, transfers, funding, payouts and trading activity.
  • Device & usage data — IP address, device and app information, and logs used for security and fraud prevention.

3. How we use it

We use your data to provide and operate our services, verify your identity, process payments and trades, prevent fraud and money laundering, meet legal and regulatory obligations, provide support, and improve our products. We rely on contract performance, legal obligation, legitimate interests and (where applicable) your consent as our lawful bases.

4. Identity & BVN data

When you verify your identity, we submit your details (including BVN) to licensed verification and payment partners to confirm your identity and enable features such as a dedicated receiving account. We do not retain your raw BVN beyond what is required to complete verification; we keep only the verification result and the limited identity attributes needed to operate your account and meet record-keeping rules.

5. Who we share with

We share data with our regulated banking and payment partners, identity-verification and KYC providers, fraud-prevention and analytics providers, and with regulators, courts or law-enforcement where legally required. We require partners to protect your data and to use it only for the purposes we specify. We do not sell your personal data.

6. Cookies

Our websites and apps use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember preferences, secure your session and measure usage. Strictly necessary cookies are required for the service to work. You can control non-essential cookies through your browser settings; disabling some cookies may affect functionality.

7. Retention

We keep personal and transaction data for as long as your account is active and thereafter for the periods required by financial-services and tax record-keeping rules (typically several years), after which we delete or anonymise it.

8. Your rights

Subject to law, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, object to or restrict certain processing, and request portability. Some rights are limited where we must retain data to meet legal obligations (for example, AML record-keeping). To exercise your rights, contact us using the details below.

9. Security

We protect your data with encryption in transit, PIN and biometric/passkey authentication, access controls, monitoring and fraud detection. No system is perfectly secure; please keep your credentials safe and tell us immediately of any suspected compromise.

10. Contact

For privacy requests or questions, contact our data protection contact at privacy@torama.money or contact@torama.money.

This Policy is a general template tailored to Torama's products. Please have qualified legal counsel and your Data Protection Officer review and adapt it — including the registered entity, partner names and retention periods — before relying on it.
